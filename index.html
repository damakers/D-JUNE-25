BELIEVING

Unexpected Surfer

I founded my personal Brand SURFING DA UNEXPECTED in 2020 in the beginning of the Pandemic Times.

SDU has a special meaning for me. Although I am not a surfer, I adore the sea and the animals. I am a faithful swimmer. The sea life has taught me so many lessons.

Unexpected Events have become in a figurateve way like the Big Waves of the Sea. In a metaphorical way I can say: "We have the power to ride them out! The control to Surf them out! To find Joy and Enjoyment with them!"

For me, Surfing stands for Accepting.

Past years have been tough for everybody but also a lot of personal growth. We became more flexible, competent, adaptable and ready to Accept the Unexpected. My personal Brand SURFING DA UNEXPECTED takes in heart the meaning of those words.

My strongest motivation lies in serving others, understanding their needs, and guiding them toward meaningful personal and professional outcomes.

I am happy I have become an Unexpected Surfer for Life.

Greetings from Botswana,

Damaris Gomez

How I Help You To Boost Your Brand

8 Effective Strategies For Creating A Distinctive Brand Identity

Conduct Market Research

This involves gathering information about your target audience, including their demographics, preferences, and behaviors. Your audience will connect with your brand.

Define Your Brand's Purpose and Values

This involves identifying what your brand stands for, what it believes in, and what it hopes to achieve. We can create a brand identity that resonates with your target audience and sets you apart from the competition.

Develop a Unique Brand Voice

Are you playful and fun, or serious and professional? Are you quirky and unconventional, or traditional and dependable? Once you’ve identified your brand’s personality, use it consistently across all your marketing channel.

Tell a Compelling Brand Story

Your brand story should communicate your company’s values, mission, and unique selling proposition and include how and why your business was started. It should also be authentic and resonate with your target audience.

Create a Strong Visual Identity

Logo, color scheme, typography, and other visual elements that represent your brand. Be consistent across all of your marketing materials, including your website, social media profiles, and advertising campaigns.

Create Compelling Content

This includes everything from blog posts and social media updates to videos and podcasts. By creating high-quality, engaging content, you can establish your brand as a thought leader in your industry and build a loyal following of customers.

Emphasize Customer Experience

Personalize your interactions with customers and use feedback to improve your products or services. A positive customer experience can create brand loyalty and help your business stand out from competitors.

Emphasize Your Unique Selling Proposition

This could be a specific feature or benefit of your product or service, or it could be your company’s values or mission. Make sure your USP is clear and consistent across all your marketing channels.
